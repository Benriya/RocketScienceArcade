--Starting--														
Double click to home.html to start the app.							
																	
GAME1, GAME2, GAME3 either will lead you into the game.				
EXIT button will open google in case you are struggling				
with the game and you want to search for cheats.					
																	
--Controlls--														
Mouse: 				move your ship									
Left Mouse Button: 	Shoot											
																	
--Goal--															
Shoot as many enemies as you are able to.							
You will die if any enemy caches you, and the game will restart.
